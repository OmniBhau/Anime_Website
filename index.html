The Origin

Where Darkness
Becomes
Legend

"In the age before memory, the crows carried the souls of fallen warriors across the void between worlds."

Karasu began as a whispered myth — a forbidden scroll describing a secret war between ancient crow-spirit clans and the empire of light. What started as legend has evolved into the defining animated universe of a generation.

The Lore Timeline

Year 0 · The Founding

The First Crow Awakens

Ryū opens his eyes at the center of a dead star. The void remembers his name first. Five crows circle him — each a future clan.

Year 340 · The Fracture

The Covenant is Broken

The third clan betrays the Covenant during the Night of Falling Stars. Reality cracks. The Void bleeds into the living world for the first time.

Year 612 · The Eclipse Wars

Luminant Order Rises

Formed in direct opposition to crow dominion. Their weapons are made of condensed daylight — the only thing that can kill a shadow sovereign.

Year 999 · The Present

The Final Eclipse Approaches

Prophecy speaks of a black sun. Every clan prepares. Shirogane sees all three outcomes — none are peace.
